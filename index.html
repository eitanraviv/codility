The Codility repo is an effort to share solutions to computer science problems with the developer community. Since I am also very interested in clean and beautiful code, I have made an effort to show that it is possible to solve these kinds of problems while maintaining high quality, easily understandable code. I feel that this type of code promotes education and reuse which are the corner stones of better craftsmanship and integrity in our trade.
